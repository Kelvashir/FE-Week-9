Board Game Tournament

Calling all board game enthusiasts! We are excited to announce our first annual Board Game Tournament. This tournament is open to players of all skill levels, and will feature a variety of popular board games.
Signup
The tournament will be held in October. Players will be divided into groups and will compete in a round-robin format. The player with the best record at the end of the round will win the group. The winners of each group will then advance to the playoffs. The playoffs will be a single-elimination tournament, with the winner of the final match being crowned the champion of the tournament. The prize for the winner of the tournament is a trophy.
